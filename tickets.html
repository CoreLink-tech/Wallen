Before You Open Listings

What am I looking at in the hall list?

These hall groupings mirror the way resale shoppers usually start: broad seating lanes first, then filtered listings once they know the section style they want.

Why is the listings page separate now?

The overview page focuses on venue layout, event context, and hall pricing bands. The separate listings page handles the actual order cards, filters, and checkout handoff.

Does the selected hall carry into listings?

Yes. When you use the listings button from this page, the selected quantity and hall filter move with you so the listing page opens in the right context.
